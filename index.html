How ChairUp Works

The smartest way to access professional space — or monetize the space you have.

For Service Providers

Book a seat & grow your clientele

01

Create your free profile

Tell us your specialty — haircuts, color, nails, lashes, massage, threading, and more. Upload your portfolio and set your rates.

02

Search available spaces

Filter by location, date, space type, price, and amenities. See real-time availability and verified photos.

03

Instant booking

Book by the hour, half-day, or full day. Payment is held securely until after your session.

04

Show up & get paid

Walk in with your tools, serve your clients, collect your earnings. No long-term obligations.

For Shop Owners

Earn from your empty chairs

01

List your space

Add photos, describe your amenities (WiFi, shampoo bowl, towels, parking), set your daily or hourly rate.

02

Set your availability

Control exactly when your chairs are open. Block out your busy times and open slots when you're slow.

03

Approve bookings

Instant book or review every renter. Our verification system screens all service professionals.

04

Get paid automatically

Funds are deposited within 24 hours of each completed booking. No chasing payments.
